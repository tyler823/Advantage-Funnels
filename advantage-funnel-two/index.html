Two Things Most Members Don't Realize About Their Credit

The credit model is already compelling on paper. These two features make it something else entirely — a financial instrument that works for you across properties and across events, not just once.

Moving? Your Credit Goes With You.

Your accumulated restoration credit is not anchored to a single address. Upon verified relocation, your full credit balance transfers to your new property — so the months or years you've spent building don't vanish because you moved. Whether you're upsizing, downsizing, or relocating across the state, your credit travels with your membership.

The Mechanism

Exactly How Your Credit Works

Every step is designed to put money back in your pocket when you need it, and protection in your home while you don't.

01

You Pay Your Monthly Subscription

Your monthly payment ($119, $199, or $299 depending on plan) is processed and 100% of that amount is logged as accumulated restoration credit in your account.

Example: Month 1 of Advanced Plan → $199 credited to your account immediately.
02

Credit Grows Every Month

There is no cap on how much credit you can accumulate while your membership is active. The longer you stay a member, the more protection value you hold.

Example: After 12 months at $199/mo → $2,388 waiting to be used.
03

Credit Applied to a Covered Event

If a covered water, fire, or storm event occurs at your home and Mr. Restore performs the mitigation or reconstruction, your full accumulated credit is applied directly to the bill — reducing or eliminating your out-of-pocket cost.

Example: $2,388 credit applied to a $4,500 mitigation job → you pay $2,112 instead of $4,500.
